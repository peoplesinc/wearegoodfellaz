Driven Entities, LLC, dba Goodfellaz have been planning and hosting events for the young professional crowd in the Baltimore Metropolitan area since 2009. Driven Entities LLC, began with four driven individuals, Taavon Peoples, Sheldon Leigh, Kareem Garrison, and Anthony Barnes as its founding members. The company’s mission is to provide upscale entertainment in a family like environment. Come to a Goodfellaz event and you’ll be treated like we’ve known you forever.

Driven Entities is determined to be a beacon in the community and have shown that through several charitable donations, included but not limited to a yearly fund raiser for the American Cancer Society.
